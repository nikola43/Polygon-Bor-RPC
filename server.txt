  My Recommendation

  Hetzner AX162-S is the sweet spot:

  - 48 cores = overkill for your needs (great headroom)
  - 128 GB RAM = perfect for GOMEMLIMIT=56GiB + 1000 peers + OS
  - 7.68 TB NVMe = fits initial data + ~3 months growth
  - $221/mo with unlimited bandwidth
  - Add extra NVMe later if you need more runway

  Important: Use JBOD (no RAID) and mount both drives. Put Bor data on one, Heimdall on the other. Or use RAID-0 (stripe) for max
  IOPS if you're comfortable re-syncing from snapshot on drive failure.
